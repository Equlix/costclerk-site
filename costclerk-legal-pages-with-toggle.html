What is CostClerk?

CostClerk is a subscription-based invoice automation service for contractors in Malaysia. You forward PDF invoices to our email address, and we send back per-project cost breakdowns as CSV reports. The entire service runs over email — there is no app, dashboard, or login.

This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, who we share it with, and how you can request deletion. It is written in compliance with Malaysian data protection law.

By forwarding invoices and subscribing to CostClerk, you consent to the collection, processing, and cross-border transfer of your data as described in this policy.

What data do we collect?

From you directly: Your email address, which is used solely to identify your account, receive your invoices, and send your reports. It is not used for marketing, profiling, or any other purpose.

From the invoices you forward to us: The PDF invoices you send may contain supplier names, pricing and cost details, project names or descriptions, site or delivery addresses, SSM registration numbers, and client names. We process this information solely to generate your cost breakdown reports.

Why do we collect it?

We collect and process your data for the following purposes only:

To operate CostClerk — receiving your invoices, parsing them, generating reports, and emailing them back to you.

To manage your account — maintaining your email on our whitelist so CostClerk recognises your invoices.

To identify common formatting patterns that help us improve parsing accuracy.

We do not use your data for marketing, advertising, or profiling. We do not sell or rent your data to anyone.

Who do we share it with?

To operate CostClerk, we use the following third-party services. Your data is shared with them only to the extent necessary to operate CostClerk:

Anthropic (United States)

We use the Anthropic Claude API to parse the content of your invoices. Invoice text and images are sent to Anthropic's servers for processing. CostClerk uses the Anthropic API under commercial terms. Data sent to the API is not used by Anthropic to train their AI models. Anthropic's data handling practices are described in their privacy policy at anthropic.com/privacy.

Stripe (United States)

We use Stripe to process subscription payments. Stripe processes your email address and payment card details. CostClerk does not store your card information. Stripe's privacy policy is available at stripe.com/privacy.

Resend (United States)

We use Resend to deliver report emails to you. Resend processes your email address and the email content (including attached reports) for delivery.

Google Workspace (Global)

Our incoming email (where you forward invoices) is hosted on Google Workspace. Google processes incoming emails in accordance with their privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

DigitalOcean (Singapore)

Our servers and database are hosted on DigitalOcean's Singapore datacenter. DigitalOcean stores and processes the data on our servers as our infrastructure provider.

These are the only third-party services that handle your data.

Cross-border data transfers

Some of the third-party services listed above are based outside Malaysia. This means your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside Malaysia, including the United States and Singapore. These transfers are necessary to provide the CostClerk service to you.

By using CostClerk, you consent to these transfers. All data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) to and from these services. We select service providers that maintain reasonable security standards for the protection of your data.

How long do we keep your data?

We do not retain invoice data indefinitely. Processed invoice data and reports are periodically deleted from our systems.

Your email address is retained for as long as your subscription is active. If you cancel your subscription, your email is removed from our active systems when your subscription ends. It may remain in backups for up to 30 days.

How do we protect your data?

We take reasonable steps to protect your data from unauthorised access, loss, misuse, or disclosure. These measures include:

Encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) for all data in transit.

Access to our servers is restricted and protected by SSH authentication.

Database access is limited to the CostClerk application only.

No CostClerk staff other than the sole operator have access to your data.

Our servers generate operational logs that may contain your email address. These are used for troubleshooting and are not shared with third parties.

Cookies and analytics

costclerk.com does not use cookies or tracking analytics.

Under Malaysian data protection law, you have the right to:

Access — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

Correction — Request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete data.

Withdrawal of consent — Withdraw your consent for us to process your data at any time. This will result in termination of your CostClerk subscription, as we cannot operate the service without processing invoice data.

Deletion — Request that we delete all your personal data from our systems.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@costclerk.com. We will respond within 21 days.

By forwarding these invoices, you confirm that you have the right to share this information with CostClerk for the purpose of generating your cost reports.
